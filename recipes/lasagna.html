Lasagna

This is what you're working toward.

A classic italian staple dish, lasagna is, in this home-chef's opinion, the ultimate comfort food. It is not super quick to make, but it won't break the bank, and it won't disappoint.

Ingredients
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Parmigano Regiano chese (grated)
  • Meat sauce for pasta dishes (several options here, see Pasta Sauce recipes)
  • Flat-leaf parsley, coarsely chopped
Steps
  1. Prep a 9x12 inch casserole dish
  2. Preheat oven to 400 deg. F
  3. Layer Pasta, sauce and Ricotta & Mozarella cheeses
  4. Bake for 30-40 mins. until cheese on top is starting to bubble and brown
  5. Let rest for 10 mins before cutting to serve
  6. Garnish with freshly grated Parmigano Regiano and parsley right before serving
